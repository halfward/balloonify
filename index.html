FAQ

How does BALLOONIFY! work?

BALLOONIFY! involves placing an inflating balloon inside a room. The balloon expands to reshape and renovate the space. It can be customized with various materials and colors and includes features like ambient lighting and smart home integration.


Is BALLOONIFY! safe to use?

Yes, BALLOONIFY! is designed with high safety standards. It complies with the US East Coast Federal Government's Adaptive Reuse Act and is used in many LEED Obsidian certified projects. It includes advanced safety sensors and a built-in power supply for secure and reliable operation.


Can BALLOONIFY! be used in historic buildings?

Yes, BALLOONIFY! is perfect for preserving and enhancing historic buildings. Our technology complies with the US East Coast Federal Government's Adaptive Reuse Act and the NY Housing Act for Building Reuse, ensuring that historic structures are treated with care while being transformed into modern, functional spaces. For more information on the US East Coast Federal Government's Adaptive Reuse Act or the NY Housing Act for Building Reuse, please contact your local government office or visit the official government website.


How do I maintain my BALLOONIFY! setup?

Regular maintenance is essential for the safe and effective operation of BALLOONIFY! We recommend inspecting the balloon periodically for any signs of wear or damage. Follow our maintenance guidelines provided with your subscription to ensure longevity and optimal performance. Failure to maintain the balloon properly may void the warranty.


What if I experience an inflation emergency?

In the event of an unexpected inflation emergency, follow the Emergency Deflation Protocol included in your BALLOONIFY! kit. Our 24/7 support team is also available to assist with any inflating or deflating needs. Ensure all occupants and pets are safely outside during the deflation process.
